How Tie & Dye is Made

1. Choose a cotton fabric.
2. Fold, twist or crumple it.
3. Tie with rubber bands.
4. Apply colorful dyes.
5. Let it dry.
6. Wash and remove the ties.
7. Enjoy the beautiful design!
